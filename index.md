# Programme Insights — User Documentation

Welcome to the Programme Insights user guide. These pages walk you through every part of the platform, from connecting your first documents to running assessments, reviewing findings, and generating reports.

## New here?

Start with **[Getting Started](/sops/00-getting-started)** — it covers the six-step assessment flow and gets you to your first result.

## Find your way around

- **Getting Started** — your account, workspace, and the assessment flow
- **Working with Documents** — connecting documents and browsing frameworks
- **Running Assessments** — creating and monitoring assessments
- **Reviewing Results** — understanding results, reviewing findings, generating content
- **Reporting** — assessment summaries, reports, and exports
- **Collaboration** — review queue, chat, and actions
- **Organisation** — initiatives and version comparison
- **Administration** — configuration for admins

Use the sidebar to jump to any topic, or the search box to find a specific feature.
